Wanderset Returns Policy

We will provide you with your choice of a full refund or store credit for returns made within 30 days of your receipt of the order. Returns made after 30 days may be accepted at our discretion, and, if accepted, will receive a store credit.

  • Items must be returned unworn, undamaged and unused with all tags attached.
  • Items cannot have been washed or altered in any way.
  • Footwear and accessories must be returned in their original packaging, including dust bags, where provided, and must be shipped inside another box to prevent damage.
