If you sell through Magento, you can start selling on Wanderset in seconds.
We make connecting your Magento store to Wanderset a snap.

how it works

  • We automatically load your Magento product inventory into Wanderset and keep it updated in realtime.
  • When customers order your products on Wanderset, orders appear in your Magento account.
  • Ship your orders directly to the customer and collect payment from Wanderset.

how to connect your store with wanderset

  • To get started, go to your Magento store and login as an admin.
  • After you see your admin dashboard, click on System > Integrations
  • When the integrations page opens, click on "Add New Integration" in the top right corner and enter the following information in text boxes.
  • Click on API in the left sidebar and choose all from the "Resource Access" dropdown.
  • After that just click on "Save and activate" and on "Allow" button.
  • Wait couple of seconds and when the popup opens enter the name your store will be labeled with and click on "Sumbit".
