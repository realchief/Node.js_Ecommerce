If you sell through Magento, you can start selling on Wanderset in seconds.
We make connecting your Magento store to Wanderset a snap.

how it works

  • We automatically load your Magento product inventory into Wanderset and keep it updated in realtime.
  • When customers order your products on Wanderset, orders appear in your Magento account.
  • Ship your orders directly to the customer and collect payment from Wanderset.

how to connect your store with wanderset

  • To get started, go to your Magento store, log in as an admin and click on "System" in the sidebar.
  • Then click on "Integrations".
  • When the integrations page opens, click on "Add New Integration".
  • In the new integration form, fill in the following data:
    Name: Desired name for the integration
    Callback URL: https://wanderset.com/magento/authorize
    Identity link URL: https://wanderset.com/magento/connect
    Password: Your Magento password to authorize the integration
  • Click on API in the left sidebar and choose all from the "Resource Access" dropdown.
  • After that just click on "Save and activate" and on "Allow" button.
  • Wait a couple of seconds and when the popup opens enter the name of your store.
  • Click on submit and wait until the popup closes.
  • Go back to the integrations page and if you see the status "Active" next to the Wanderset integration, you've successfully integrated your store with Wanderset.
