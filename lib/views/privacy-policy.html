WANDERSET CUSTOMER PRIVACY POLICY

Thank you for visiting Wanderset.com. We respect your privacy and protecting your personal information is very important to us.

Wanderset, LLC owns and operates Wanderset.com and Wanderset mobile and tablet applications (together, these may be referred to below as “Wanderset”, “we”, “us”, “our” or “the site”). This Privacy Policy applies to Wanderset and all the aforementioned entities.

When you shop on Wanderset, we obtain information we need to complete your purchase or other transaction. This page informs you of our Privacy Policy related to the collection, use and disclosure of such personal information, along with any other additional information you provide.

Security

We maintain physical, electronic and procedural safeguards to protect the confidentiality and security of the non-public personal information you transmit to us.

To help protect your information, we use Secure Sockets Layer technology (SSL). SSL encrypts your information before it is sent to us, so only we can decode it. Make sure to confirm that your connection is secure (especially if you are using public Wi-Fi) prior to submitting any private information. You will know that your information is being encrypted if the URL begins with https:// (rather than http://) and you see a closed lock in your browser window.

Information Collected

For purposes of this policy, personal information can include your name, address (city, state, zip, country), email address and phone number. Additional information may include your product purchases and other transactions, your shipping preferences, any other opt-in information you choose to save, or other information, such as your birthday, you may submit if you register with us. In addition, like most sites, we use cookies, to collect non-personal information to help improve our overall site experience.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are sent to your browser from a website you visit. We may use cookies to identify your device, your browser type and version, your operating system and general location information. Cookies also help us conduct fraud prevention services and understand how users navigate the site.

Use and Disclosure of Information

We use the personal information that you transmit to us to process and complete your transactions with us, communicate with you, respond to your comments, notify you of product recalls or safety issues, process your participation in our programs, including selling on Wanderset, and personalize your experiences if you ask us to. We also use the information for service improvements, analytics, operations, fraud detection and prevention, reporting and legal compliance.

We may use carefully selected third-parties, such as Google Analytics, to monitor and analyze your cookies data to improve your shopping experience, and the product and content assortment offered on the site.

If you choose to receive marketing and promotional materials from Wanderset, we will use your personal information to send them to you. If you no longer wish to receive the same, you will be able to unsubscribe in every email, as well as adjust your settings in our email preferences center.

Opt Out Procedures

If you would prefer that we not share your personal information other parties, such as Google Analytics, you have the right to opt-out. Please contact us at Wanderset, LLC., Attn. Policies Dept., 400 Stuart Street, 19th floor, Boston, MA 02116 or email support@wanderset.com.

Updating Personal Information

You have the right to review and update or change your personal details on Wanderset. If you have registered online, you may do so at any time by logging in to your account.

Children’s Privacy

Wanderset is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We will never knowingly request or collect any personal information from children under 13 without prior verifiable consent from a parent or guardian. If we become aware that anyone under 13 years of age has submitted any information to Wanderset without prior verifiable parental consent, we will delete all their information.

Changes to the Policy

We may update or make changes to our Privacy Policy from time to time and such updates will be communicated by updating this page. To the extent that any such updates or changes will materially affect the way we use or disclose your Personal Information, we will notify you by the email address we have for you, if you have registered your email with us.

Additional Information

If Wanderset links to unaffiliated third party websites. Our Privacy Policy does not apply to these sites.

Measures You Can Take to Protect Your Privacy

Please consider the following additional steps you can take to help protect your personal information online: install the latest anti-virus software, do not use the same password across multiple sites, use a complex password and password protect your mobile device(s).
